SMS Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to SMS messages sent by the Clay Liddell SMS assistant, including messages sent when a caller requests a Liddell Stor-All or Liddell's Rentals payment, login, rental, or customer support link.

Information We Collect

When you request an SMS message, we may collect your phone number, caller ID, the date and time of the request, message delivery status, and related technical logs needed to send and troubleshoot the message.

How We Use Information

We use this information to send the SMS message you requested, provide customer support, monitor delivery, prevent misuse, maintain security, and comply with legal or carrier requirements.

Sharing

We may share information with service providers that help deliver messages, such as telecommunications providers, cloud hosting providers, and related business systems needed to support the requested message. We may also share information if required by law.

We do not sell or rent your mobile phone number. We do not share mobile opt-in information or consent with third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes.

Your Choices

You can opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP. You can request help by replying HELP. Message and data rates may apply.

Data Retention and Security

We keep SMS-related records only as long as reasonably needed for support, auditing, security, and compliance. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect the information we maintain.

Contact

For questions about this SMS Privacy Policy, visit clayliddell.com.
